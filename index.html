FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We have answered a wide range of Questions for your Convenience

1. Can you make my Shopify site faster?

Yes, sure! I have a lot of satisfied clients, so I can dramatically improve your website performance. Also, I employ my own methods to ensure that Shopify will be much faster than “out of the box”..

2. What kind of web services do you use to measure speed?

Google Insights, GTmetrix, Pingdom tools

3. What's your guarantee?

I guarantee a Google PageSpeed Insights score of at least 70 for mobile versions, and at least 80 for desktop versions. GTmetrix shows full page loading; a value of 2-6 seconds is good. Page loading on Pingdom is different; here, 1 or 2 seconds is an appropriate target.

4. Scores vs user experience?

Scores don’t always reflect the best settings; the most important factor is the user experience. I have a lot of clients and projects, so I’m in an excellent position to establish the best balance between scores and user experience.

5. Can you tell me about your process?

You’ll be assigned a developer to take the lead on your project. I backup your live theme and do all the work on this new, duplicated theme. I will communicate with you throughout the project. At the end of the project, I will provide you with a report showing an overview of what we did, together with before/after results.

6. How long does this take?

It can usually be done in 7–9 business days. Sometimes there’s a queue, in which case I'll let you know the estimated waiting time.

7. Can I make changes to the site while you are working on it?

It’s best to have only one person working on the site at a time. I work on a duplicated theme, so if you make any changes to your live theme while I'm working, I will have to apply those edits to our new theme as well.

8. Are the changes you make future-proof?

I try to future-proof as much as possible by editing the Liquid theme code and using JavaScript when applicable. Generally, the edits I perform are long-lasting. I also include recommendations in the report to give you relevant information for the future.

9. Does your optimization work cover mobile, desktop, or both?

My optimization work covers most of your site and all devices. Regardless of the device accessing your site, it’s the same code base, just styled differently according to the screen size.

10. Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. I recommend checking the site every month to keep the speed as high as possible. Of course, we can negotiate about future support; my rate is $99/hour.
